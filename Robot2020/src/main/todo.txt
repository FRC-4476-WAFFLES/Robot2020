Climber > Shooter

Default state:
    - Default intake position is in.
    - Hood is down by default.
    - Climber lock ratchet engaged by default, only disengaging when climbing.

Interferences:
    - Climber deploy interferes with intake.
        - Beginning of match:
            - Intake out
            - Climber down (zero ?)
        - Start deploy:
            - Intake out
            - Climber up
        - Undeploy
            - Intake out
            - Climber down
    - Climber lock must be on while shooting and intaking.

Shooter has a speedup motor on a talon/victor. Run back while intaking.
